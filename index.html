What is this Site?

This page is meant to provide a brief example of a Mutants and Masterminds game, using rules in the third edition of the game, to demonstrate variant rules for Hit Points rather than Toughness saves.

Designer Templates

Using GitHub to create a launching point for the site, and combined efforts from two people who enjoy both working on programs such as this one as well as streamlining the actual gameplay of RPGs, the authors of this particular page welcome you to enjoy the fruits of their labors and run a simple simulation, using revised rules of damage calculations rather than the skill checks and complex calculations used in the published ruleset.

Creating pages manually
